Elijah Foard's User Manual

My Style

I am introverted and quiet in large group settings but talkative in one-on-one situations. I usually won’t step into situations unless I am directly asked. I can actually talk quite a bit about random topics that I have a large knowledge of for no particular reason.


My Values

I value getting my work done as soon as possible and as best as I can. I typically sit down and get my work done in one sitting so I can have that feeling of accomplishment.


When to approach me

The best time to approach me would be while I am not busy and while I’m with somebody I know


How to best communicate with me

Usually directly talking to me is less stressful than phone calls or texts because I can’t read tone over texts


How I make decisions

I think about the outcomes from each decision and determine which will be the best for the situation


What people misunderstand about me

I am not in a constant state of anger :)


How to help me

If I seem to be struggling with something I usually ask others for help but you can ask me directly and I will tell you if I need help or not
